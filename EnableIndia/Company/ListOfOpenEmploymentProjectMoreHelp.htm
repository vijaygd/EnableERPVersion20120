LEGEND:
OTHER ACTIVITIES YOU CAN DO ON THIS SCREEN:
To view 'Assigned List' for any Employment Project, click on 'Candidates Waiting in Assigned List' for that Project.
To view 'Interview List' for any Employment Project, click on 'Candidates Waiting in Interview List' for that Project.
To view 'Successful Candidates List' for any Employment Project, click on 'Successful Candidates' for that Project.
Delete an Employment Project by selecting it using radio-button and click on 'Delete Employment Project' button.
To view and update Parent Company Details, click on Parent Company Name.
To view and update Company Details, click on Company Name.
To update details about Vacancy Code, click on Vacancy Code.
To update details of the Employment Project, click on Employment Projects Code.
